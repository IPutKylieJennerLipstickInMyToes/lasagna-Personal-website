Welcome to the Habitation Nation!

Learn about endangered species and how you can help protect them.

Anybody from across the nation can volunteer to step in and help all the different types of species that are endangered. Here at the Habitation Nation, we are looking for dedicated volunteers who are passionate about contributing in every possible manner to the preservation of endangered animals and the restoration of their natural habitats. Our ideal volunteers are comitted individuals who enthusiastically support initiatives aimed at safeguarding wildlife and enhancing ecological sustainability.

Keeping this up, the endangered species of America will be repopulated and put into great habitats. :)
